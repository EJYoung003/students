Network Administrator/Analyst

The Network Administrator/Analyst program at Hennepin Technical College prepares you to enter the field of Network Administrations with skills needed to manage a network. You learn about how computers work, how to keep computers and networks secure, and how networks operate.
I became interested in Network Administration while studying at Hennepin Technical College in the area of Photography. They are very different areas of study, but I have interests in both the hobby of photography and computer science. I felt that studying in the areas of computers would be of a greater benefit for me in the long run.
